What is DuoQue?

Duoque is a matching platform for hobby partners, similar to Tinder, where users connect based on shared interests and activities. Finding hobby partners can be challenging when using most social media platforms, as they primarily cater to dating or professional networking. DuoQue aims to bridge the gap by connecting people based on their shared hobbies. Whether it's reading, gaming, painting, or surfing, our app will provide a swipe-based, geosocial matchmaking system that makes it easier to find a compatible partner. By using innovative matchmaking algorithms, our platform will ensure that users are matched based on their location and shared interest to create meaningful connections.
