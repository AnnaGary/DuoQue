What is DuoQue?

Duoque is a matching platform for hobby partners, similar to Tinder, where users connect based on shared interests and activities. Finding hobby partners can be challenging when using most social media platforms, as they primarily cater to dating or professional networking. DuoQue bridges the gap by connecting people based on shared hobbies. Whether it's reading, gaming, painting, or surfing, our app provides a swipe-based, geosocial matchmaking system that makes it easier to find a compatible partner.
